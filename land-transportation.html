Reliable Land Transportation Solutions

At Dynamic Shipping Solutions, we provide comprehensive land transportation services that ensure your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time. Our ground transport solutions cover domestic deliveries, cross-border shipping, and intermodal connections.

A full range of trucking and intermodal services provided by the group companies through selected and qualified sub-contractors i.e. city pick up as well as long distance transportation, ensuring complete door-to-door solutions for your logistics needs.

Our Land Transportation Services Include:

Full Truck Load (FTL)

Dedicated trucks for large shipments

Less Than Truck Load (LTL)

Shared truck space for smaller shipments

Door-to-Door Delivery

Complete pickup and delivery solutions

Cross-Border Transport

International land transportation services

Vehicle Types Available:

  • Standard Trucks: General cargo transportation
  • Refrigerated Trucks: Temperature-sensitive goods
  • Flatbed Trucks: Oversized and heavy cargo
  • Container Trucks: Container transportation from ports
  • Specialized Vehicles: Hazardous and special cargo
  • Multi-axle Trailers: Heavy machinery and equipment

Why Choose Our Land Transportation?

  • Flexible Scheduling: Pickup and delivery as per your timeline
  • Real-time Tracking: Monitor your shipment throughout the journey
  • Qualified Drivers: Experienced and licensed professionals
  • Insurance Coverage: Comprehensive protection for your cargo
  • Cost-Effective: Competitive rates for all transportation needs
  • 24/7 Support: Round-the-clock customer service
